ngrok provides powerful mechanisms to observe and introspect the network traffic flowing through your endpoints. 
You can view real-time traffic in the dashboard with Traffic Inspector, or export events to your own logging systems for deeper analysis and monitoring.

## Features

- [Traffic Inspector](/obs/traffic-inspection/) - Real-time view of HTTP traffic with request replay capabilities.
- [Event Exporting](/obs/events/) - Subscribe to log sources and publish events to external destinations.
- [Traffic Events](/obs/events/reference/#traffic-events) - Capture logs when traffic transits through your endpoints.
- [Audit Events](/obs/events/reference/#audit-events) - Track changes to your account configuration and resources.
