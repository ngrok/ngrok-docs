AI Gateway credits are a prepaid balance that funds [AI Gateway API Key](/ai-gateway/concepts/api-keys) usage. When you [bring your own keys](/ai-gateway/bring-your-own-keys/overview), credits are not required—you pay providers directly through your own billing relationship.

## Plan requirement

AI Gateway requires the **Pay-as-you-go** plan. Free and Hobbyist plans cannot use the AI Gateway.

## Purchasing credits

[Purchase credits](https://dashboard.ngrok.com/ai-gateways/credit/add) directly from the ngrok dashboard. The minimum purchase is USD $5.00.

Credits expire **one year** after purchase. There is no REST API for purchasing credits—this is a dashboard-only operation.

## How credits are consumed

When you use [AI Gateway API Keys](/ai-gateway/concepts/api-keys), credits cover both ngrok's processing fee and the upstream provider cost (OpenAI, Anthropic). ngrok pays the provider on your behalf.

When you [bring your own keys](/ai-gateway/bring-your-own-keys/overview), credits are not required—you pay providers directly through your own billing relationship.

## Balance tracking

View your current credit balance in the [ngrok dashboard](https://dashboard.ngrok.com/ai-gateways).

## Low balance alerts

When your balance drops below a threshold, ngrok sends an email notification warning you to purchase additional credits before they run out.

## What happens when credits run out

When your credit balance reaches zero:

1. **AI Gateway API Key requests stop working**—ngrok can no longer pay providers on your behalf
2. Clients receive **HTTP 403** with error [`ERR_NGROK_4026`](/errors/err_ngrok_4026): "Your account has run out of credit"

<Note>
BYOK requests are not affected by credit depletion — you pay providers directly through your own billing relationship.
</Note>

## Restoring access

[Purchase additional credits](https://dashboard.ngrok.com/ai-gateways/credit/add) to restore access. Once your balance is restored, AI Gateway API Keys resume working immediately.

## AI Gateway credits vs. platform credits

AI Gateway credits are separate from general ngrok platform credits (used for endpoints, bandwidth, and more on Free/Hobbyist plans). They are:

They are purchased separately, tracked separately, and used exclusively for AI Gateway requests.
