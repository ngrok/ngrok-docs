### Message

You are not allowed to create endpoints with subdomains on `BASE`. Your account is on the `PLAN` plan, which doesn't allow starting tunnels on `BASE`.

If you're having trouble resolving this error, please reach out to [support@ngrok.com](mailto:support@ngrok.com?subject=Help%20with%20ERR_NGROK_15000)
