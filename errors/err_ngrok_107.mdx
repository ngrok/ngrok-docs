### Message

The authtoken you specified is properly formed, but it is invalid.
Your authtoken: `TOKEN`
This usually happens when: - You reset your authtoken - Your authtoken was for a team account that you were removed from - You are using ngrok link and this credential was explicitly revoked
Go to your ngrok dashboard and double check that your authtoken is correct:
https://dashboard.ngrok.com/get-started/your-authtoken
