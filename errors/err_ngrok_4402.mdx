### Message

The invited user would exceed your current team size limit of `TEAM_SIZE`. Add another seat at: https://dashboard.ngrok.com/billing/subscription. Then you'll be able to invite your team.

If you're having trouble resolving this error, please reach out to [support@ngrok.com](mailto:support@ngrok.com?subject=Help%20with%20ERR_NGROK_4402)
