### Message

You must reserve a custom hostname for your account before it can be used to create endpoints.
Failed to create an endpoint with the domain `DOMAIN` for the account `ACCOUNT`'.

Reserve this name on your dashboard: https://dashboard.ngrok.com/domains/new
