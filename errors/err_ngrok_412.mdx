### Message

Only accounts on paid plans can reserve endpoints on custom domains.
Your account cannot reserve endpoints on custom domains (`DOMAIN` is not a subdomain of `BASE`).

Upgrade to a paid plan at: https://dashboard.ngrok.com/billing/choose-a-plan

If you're having trouble resolving this error, please reach out to [support@ngrok.com](mailto:support@ngrok.com?subject=Help%20with%20ERR_NGROK_412)
