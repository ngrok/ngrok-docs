# ERR_NGROK_412

### Message

Only Personal, Pro, Enterprise, or Pay-as-you-go accounts can reserve tunnels on custom domains.
Your account cannot reserve tunnels on custom domains (`DOMAIN` is not a subdomain of `BASE`).

Upgrade to a paid plan at: https://dashboard.ngrok.com/billing/subscription
