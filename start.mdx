ngrok is the front door to your applications and services.

<span className='text-xl'>***Environment Independent***</span>

ngrok can deliver traffic to services running anywhere–from AWS or on-premises Kubernetes clusters to a Raspberry Pi or your laptop–with no changes to your environment's networking.

<div className='flex'>
<div className='m-0 mr-1.5 w-auto flex place-items-center'><Icon icon="globe" size={20} /></div><span className='text-xl'>***Universal Gateway***</span>
</div>

ngrok serves as a reverse proxy, load balancer, API gateway, firewall, content delivery network, and more, providing all the components to securely deliver traffic from the internet to your applicatons and services.

## Common use cases

<Columns cols={2}>
	<Card title="AI Gateway">
		Route requests to AI providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google through a single endpoint. If a provider fails, or your API key hits rate limits, the endpoint automatically tries another.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Put your apps & services online">
		Create an Agent Endpoint to open up public internet traffic to an app or service running on the same machine.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Traffic Policy">
		Authenticate, route, and load-balance traffic to apps and services across multiple machines using Cloud Endpoints and Traffic Policy.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Kubernetes Ingress">
		Use ngrok's Kubernetes Operator to self-service endpoints to your apps and services running in Kubernetes environments.
	</Card>
</Columns>
