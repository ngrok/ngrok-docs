title: Actions

---

In Traffic Policy, actions define what happens when a [Rule](/traffic-policy/concepts/phase-rules) on a [Phase](/traffic-policy/concepts/phases) conditions are met. 
Actions allow you to manipulate, route, and manage traffic in various ways.

Some actions can short-circuit a request and immediately return back a response, while others go to the next action in the chain.
